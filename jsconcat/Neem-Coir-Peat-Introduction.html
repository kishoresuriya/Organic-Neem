Introduction

Coir is the outside layer of husk that surrounds the shell of the coconut. The Coir peat is enriched with neem for increased protection against pests and nematodes. The Terra neem coir peat is a superior growing medium in which to grow many planets and crops.

Coir peat is enriched by neem to prevent the pest and make the added quality to the natural coir peat

Terra Neem coir peat is widely used for soil conditioner and growing medium. We provide uniform model of the coco coir peat for convenient of the users, that absorbs and holds up to ten times of water than its own weight.
