Terra Neem Soil Contains:
  • Major Nutrients like Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potash (NPK) in an organic form
  • Completely mineralized, with narrow C:N ratio
  • Contains Beneficial micro-organism for better soil health in an immobilized state.
  • Blended with Micro-nutrients and secondary nutrients like calcium and magnesium for better fertilizer use efficiency
  • Enriched with potassium as sulphate of Potash
  • Mixed with Terra neem cake
  • Available in Pellet and Granule form for the easy application
  • Most suitable for all types of soil
  • Environmentally friendly, non-toxic, compatible with soil microflora and all other chemical fertilizers

Shelf life : 18 months , If stored unopened in a room temperature.

Packing : 25 Kg / 50 Kg

Label : Certified Organic Input Label.

Method of Application:

Terra neem soil can be applied directly to the soil or in the combination with Jungle soil or with own estate crop residue manure. Profuse irrigation immediately after the application is must.
