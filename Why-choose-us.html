Terra Neem - The Synonyms of the High Quality Neem Products.

Terra neem oil and neem cake is manufactured by TCP (Temperature controlled process) method. We are not using any chemicals or solvents for extraction of neem oil, this process ensures the availability of all the active ingredients of the Neem seed’s kernel in the Terra neem oil and neem cake as it is. Terra neem oil minimum assurance of azadirachtin is 3500 ppm and Terra Neem cake azadirachtin is 1000ppm.
